Fctr (pronounced "factor") is a creative twist on the word "factor," emphasizing how key elements combine to drive secure identity verification.

The Fctr Identity Portal is a secure, all-in-one solution designed for organizations using Okta or Entra ID. It simplifies user management, verification processes, and support operations without requiring direct admin access to your IDP environment.

Fctr is ideal for IT helpdesk teams, support staff, and security-conscious organizations using Okta or Entra ID. It enables tier-1 support teams to efficiently handle user verification and identity management tasks without needing extensive IDP admin privileges, improving both operational efficiency and security.

Fctr streamlines operations by providing a unified dashboard for support teams. It consolidates user information, verification processes, and common identity management tasks into a single interface, reducing the average support call time by up to 60%. Support agents can quickly verify users, troubleshoot access issues, and resolve identity problems without toggling between multiple systems.

Fctr enhances security in multiple ways: Replaces insecure knowledge-based questions with modern MFA verification, masks sensitive user data like email addresses and IP locations, prevents unauthorized data exports and information exfiltration, enables granular permission controls for support staff, and maintains comprehensive audit logs of all actions.

Yes, Fctr offers extensive customization options. The reporting module offers support efficiency metrics (resolution times, common issues), user verification activity and statistics, audit trails of all actions taken within the portal, customizable dashboards for different stakeholders, and export capabilities for compliance reporting.

While IDP admin consoles is designed for administrators managing the entire identity infrastructure, Fctr is purpose-built for support teams with a focus on daily operational tasks.
